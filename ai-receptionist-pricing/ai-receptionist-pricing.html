Common Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Does it sound like a robot? ▼
No. Your AI receptionist uses a natural, conversational voice and is trained to handle real questions about your business. Callers often don't realize they're talking to an AI.
What happens if it can't answer something? ▼
If a caller asks something outside what your AI knows, it gracefully collects their info and flags it for you to follow up — or it can transfer the call directly to your phone.
Do I need to change my existing phone number? ▼
Nope. We can set up a dedicated number or configure your existing line to forward calls. Most clients use call forwarding so their main number stays the same.
What industries does it work for? ▼
Roofing, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, dental, real estate, landscaping, auto detailing, epoxy flooring, window cleaning, fitness, and restaurants — with more added regularly. If you're a service-based business, we've got you covered.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. If you decide it's not for you, cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again.
